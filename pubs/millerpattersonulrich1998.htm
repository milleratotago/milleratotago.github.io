• Miller, J. O., Patterson, T., & Ulrich, R. (1998). Jackknife-based method for measuring LRP onset latency differences. Psychophysiology, 35(1), 99-115. -- DOI -- PubMed -- Google Scholar -- Erratum --

    There is a typo in Table A1 on page 115: for subsample 2, the latency in the experimental condition should be 587.54 rather than 577.54. In addition, there are rounding problems, because the values in the appendix were computed without rounding to two decimal places, which had a small impact on the results. For example, computing the value of sD from the rounded, published values gives 19.339 rather than the published value of 19.42.
